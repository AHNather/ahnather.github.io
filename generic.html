About Me

Hi, I'm Ahmad Munir.

I am a dedicated Artificial Intelligence (AI) graduate from the University of Jeddah, passionate about machine learning, deep learning, and data science. My career has recently expanded into Business Intelligence, with specialized training from the Saudi Digital Academy's Business Intelligence Bootcamp in partnership with WeCloudData, where I was recognized for my work on the award-winning Luminova project in renewable energy analysis. This experience strengthened my skills in data visualization and analytics, allowing me to deliver actionable insights that drive informed decision-making.

Previously, I completed a 200-hour cooperative training at King Fahad Medical City, focusing on Microsoft Power BI, ASP.NET, and Microsoft SQL Server to support efficient data workflows. My academic and professional journey has equipped me with hands-on experience in a variety of AI and BI projects, from retail sales forecasting to quality of life analysis. This portfolio showcases projects that reflect my proficiency in using data to solve real-world challenges. I am eager to collaborate on innovative projects and continue advancing my skills in AI, data science, and business intelligence.
